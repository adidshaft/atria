# Code of Conduct

This project expects direct, technical, respectful collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Discuss evidence and tradeoffs clearly.
- Keep health and protocol claims grounded in reproducible data.
- Be respectful when disagreeing.
- Avoid sharing another person's private health data, logs, or identifiers.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private health data or device identifiers without consent.
- Misrepresenting unsupported metrics as clinically validated.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block contributors who violate these expectations.
